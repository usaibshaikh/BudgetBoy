## Release Notes for Version Code 1 (Version 1.0 Initial Release) - 

**Release Date:** 26 December 2025  
**Developer:** Mohammad Usaib

### Welcome to BudgetBoy!

BudgetBoy is your personal expense tracking companion designed to help you take control of your finances. Track your spending, identify patterns, and build better financial habits‚Äîall without internet, ads, or subscription fees.

---

## üéØ Key Features

### Main Screen
- **Smooth Launch Animation:** Enjoy a polished app experience from the moment you open BudgetBoy
- **Motivational Quotes:** Get inspired with financial wisdom displayed on the main screen to help you make smarter spending decisions
- **Clean Interface:** Beautiful cyan and teal theme for a pleasant user experience
- **Quick Access:** Two primary buttons for immediate access to core features (View Expenses & Add Expenses)

### Add Expenses
- **Simple Data Entry:** Log expense details with an intuitive interface
- **Amount Flexibility:** Enter amounts with or without decimals
- **Category Selection:** Organize expenses into predefined categories:
  - Personal Expenses
  - Phone Recharge
  - Home Expenses
  - Other
- **Automatic Timestamps:** Date and time are automatically recorded for each expense
- **Smart Input Validation:** Helpful error messages and hints prevent incomplete entries
- **Toast Notifications:** Clear feedback when input is incomplete or invalid

### View Expenses
- **Comprehensive Expense List:** See all your recorded expenses at a glance
- **Flexible Filtering:** 
  - View all expenses
  - Filter by month
  - Filter by year
- **Automatic Calculations:** Total amount spent is calculated with decimal precision for each view
- **Export Functionality:** Save expense reports to your device's download folder
- **Share Feature:** Share expense summaries via WhatsApp, email, or other apps
- **Long-Press Delete:** Remove individual expenses with a long tap (includes confirmation prompt)
- **Smart Error Handling:** Graceful messaging when no expenses exist or history is empty
- **Fast Performance:** Lightning-quick data retrieval from local storage

## üîí Privacy & Security

### Your Data, Your Device
- **100% Local Storage:** All data stays on your phone in a hidden directory
- **Zero Cloud Storage:** No data uploads, no external servers, no cloud sync
- **No Internet Required:** Works completely offline
- **No Ads:** Uninterrupted expense tracking experience
- **No Tracking:** Zero analytics, no data collection, no third-party integrations

### Data Control
- **User-Initiated Sharing:** Data only leaves your device when YOU choose to export or share
- **Easy Deletion:** Remove expenses individually or uninstall to clear all data
- **Privacy by Design:** Minimal permissions, maximum privacy

## üìù How to Use

1. **Launch the App:** Enjoy the smooth opening animation and read the motivational quote
2. **Add Expenses:** Tap "Add Expenses" ‚Üí Enter description and amount ‚Üí Select category ‚Üí Save
